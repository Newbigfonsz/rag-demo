# The Rooster (鸡 - Jī)

## Overview
The Rooster is the tenth animal in the Chinese zodiac cycle. People born in the Year of the Rooster are known for their confidence, hard work, and attention to detail. The Rooster symbolizes fidelity, punctuality, and fortune in Chinese culture.

## Years of the Rooster
- 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, 2017, 2029, 2041

## Core Personality Traits

### Positive Traits
- **Observant**: Notices details others miss
- **Hardworking**: Dedicated and industrious
- **Confident**: Strong self-assurance
- **Honest**: Direct and straightforward
- **Resourceful**: Makes the most of available resources
- **Courageous**: Willing to speak truth to power

### Challenging Traits
- **Critical**: Quick to point out flaws in others
- **Vain**: Concerned with appearance and image
- **Blunt**: Can be tactless in communication
- **Impatient**: Frustrated by inefficiency
- **Boastful**: May exaggerate accomplishments

## Element Variations

### Wood Rooster (1945, 2005)
More compassionate and team-oriented. Better emotional intelligence. Still detail-focused but more flexible. Creative problem-solvers.

### Fire Rooster (1957, 2017)
Most dynamic and trustworthy. Strong organizational skills. Can be dramatic and attention-seeking. Natural managers.

### Earth Rooster (1909, 1969)
Most reliable and systematic. Excellent with finances. Conservative approach to risk. Strong sense of responsibility.

### Metal Rooster (1921, 1981)
Most analytical and logical. Strong reasoning abilities. Can be inflexible in thinking. Determined to succeed.

### Water Rooster (1933, 1993)
More intellectual and adaptable. Better at seeing others' perspectives. Strong communication skills. Less confrontational.

## Compatibility

### Most Compatible
- **Ox**: Solid partnership built on shared work ethic
- **Snake**: Deep intellectual connection and respect
- **Dragon**: Complementary strengths and ambitions

### Least Compatible
- **Rat**: Different communication styles cause friction
- **Rabbit**: Rooster's criticism hurts sensitive Rabbit
- **Rooster**: Too critical of each other
