# The Rabbit (兔 - Tù)

## Overview
The Rabbit is the fourth animal in the Chinese zodiac cycle. People born in the Year of the Rabbit are known for their elegance, mercy, and good fortune. The Rabbit symbolizes longevity, peace, and prosperity in Chinese culture.

## Years of the Rabbit
- 1927, 1939, 1951, 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2023, 2035

## Core Personality Traits

### Positive Traits
- **Gentle**: Kind and compassionate in dealings with others
- **Elegant**: Natural grace and refined sensibilities
- **Alert**: Highly perceptive and aware of surroundings
- **Diplomatic**: Skilled at avoiding conflict and finding compromise
- **Compassionate**: Deep empathy for others' suffering
- **Artistic**: Strong aesthetic sense and creative abilities

### Challenging Traits
- **Timid**: Avoids confrontation even when necessary
- **Overly cautious**: May miss opportunities due to excessive prudence
- **Pessimistic**: Tendency to imagine worst-case scenarios
- **Detached**: Can seem aloof or emotionally distant
- **Superficial**: May prioritize appearance over substance

## Element Variations

### Wood Rabbit (1915, 1975)
More generous and empathetic. Strong desire to help others. May be taken advantage of due to kindness. Creative and imaginative.

### Fire Rabbit (1927, 1987)
More outgoing and adventurous than other Rabbits. Natural charisma and broader social circle. Can be temperamental. Leadership potential.

### Earth Rabbit (1939, 1999)
Most practical and grounded. Excellent financial instincts. Hardworking and realistic. May struggle with expressing emotions.

### Metal Rabbit (1951, 2011)
Most ambitious and determined. Strong convictions and beliefs. Can be conservative. More resilient under pressure.

### Water Rabbit (1963, 2023)
Highly sensitive and intuitive. May be easily influenced by others. Strong memory and emotional depth. Excellent at reading people.

## Compatibility

### Most Compatible
- **Goat**: Harmonious and understanding partnership
- **Pig**: Gentle and supportive relationship
- **Dog**: Loyal and stable connection

### Least Compatible
- **Rat**: Different values and communication styles
- **Dragon**: Rabbit feels overwhelmed by Dragon's intensity
- **Rooster**: Fundamental personality clashes
