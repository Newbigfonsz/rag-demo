# The Dog (狗 - Gǒu)

## Overview
The Dog is the eleventh animal in the Chinese zodiac cycle. People born in the Year of the Dog are known for their loyalty, honesty, and sense of justice. The Dog symbolizes faithfulness, protection, and integrity in Chinese culture.

## Years of the Dog
- 1934, 1946, 1958, 1970, 1982, 1994, 2006, 2018, 2030, 2042

## Core Personality Traits

### Positive Traits
- **Loyal**: Devoted to friends and family
- **Honest**: Truthful and sincere in dealings
- **Reliable**: Dependable and trustworthy
- **Courageous**: Willing to defend what's right
- **Just**: Strong sense of fairness and ethics
- **Prudent**: Careful and sensible in decisions

### Challenging Traits
- **Anxious**: Prone to worry and pessimism
- **Stubborn**: Difficulty changing mind once set
- **Critical**: Quick to judge others
- **Cynical**: Tends toward negativity and suspicion
- **Distant**: Can seem emotionally unavailable

## Element Variations

### Wood Dog (1934, 1994)
More generous and cooperative. Better at building relationships. Still loyal but more flexible. Creative and sincere.

### Fire Dog (1946, 2006)
Most charismatic and adventurous. Natural leaders with strong principles. Can be impulsive. Extremely loyal to causes.

### Earth Dog (1958, 2018)
Most practical and efficient. Better at long-term planning. Patient and reliable. Strong communication skills.

### Metal Dog (1910, 1970)
Most determined and conservative. Strong sense of duty. Can be inflexible and demanding. Highly principled.

### Water Dog (1922, 1982)
Most intuitive and flexible. Better at understanding different perspectives. Charming and liberal. Strong artistic sense.
