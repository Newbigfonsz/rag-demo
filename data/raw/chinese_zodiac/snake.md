# The Snake (蛇 - Shé)

## Overview
The Snake is the sixth animal in the Chinese zodiac cycle. People born in the Year of the Snake are known for their wisdom, intuition, and elegance. The Snake symbolizes wisdom, mystery, and divination in Chinese culture.

## Years of the Snake
- 1929, 1941, 1953, 1965, 1977, 1989, 2001, 2013, 2025, 2037

## Core Personality Traits

### Positive Traits
- **Wise**: Deep thinkers with profound insights
- **Intuitive**: Strong sixth sense and perception
- **Elegant**: Natural grace and refined taste
- **Analytical**: Excellent at dissecting complex problems
- **Determined**: Quietly persistent in achieving goals
- **Discreet**: Trustworthy keeper of secrets

### Challenging Traits
- **Secretive**: Keeps too much hidden from others
- **Suspicious**: Tends to distrust others' motives
- **Jealous**: Possessive in relationships
- **Calculating**: May manipulate situations for advantage
- **Lazy**: Can procrastinate when not motivated

## Element Variations

### Wood Snake (1905, 1965)
More open and communicative. Values close friendships. Artistic and appreciates beauty. More stable in relationships.

### Fire Snake (1917, 1977)
More active and outgoing. Natural leadership qualities. Charismatic but can be self-centered. Quick to seize opportunities.

### Earth Snake (1929, 1989)
Most grounded and reliable. Strong work ethic. Better at teamwork. Calm and collected under pressure.

### Metal Snake (1941, 2001)
Most determined and focused. Strong willpower and ambition. Can be secretive and calculating. High standards for success.

### Water Snake (1953, 2013)
Most intuitive and creative. Excellent financial instincts. Influential and persuasive. More emotionally aware.

## Compatibility

### Most Compatible
- **Ox**: Solid and stable partnership built on trust
- **Rooster**: Intellectual connection and mutual admiration
- **Dragon**: Powerful and ambitious pairing

### Least Compatible
- **Tiger**: Mutual suspicion and distrust
- **Pig**: Fundamentally different values
- **Monkey**: Too many power games and competition
