# Life Path Number 2: The Peacemaker

## Overview
Life Path 2 represents diplomacy, cooperation, and balance. People with this life path are natural mediators who bring harmony to their environments. They are meant to learn partnership and develop their intuitive abilities.

## Core Characteristics

### Strengths
- **Diplomatic**: Skilled at resolving conflicts
- **Intuitive**: Strong inner knowing and perception
- **Cooperative**: Works well with others
- **Patient**: Willing to wait for right timing
- **Sensitive**: Deeply attuned to emotions
- **Supportive**: Excellent at nurturing others

### Challenges
- **Over-sensitivity**: Easily hurt by criticism
- **Indecisiveness**: Difficulty making firm choices
- **Passivity**: May avoid necessary confrontation
- **Dependency**: Can rely too heavily on others
- **Self-doubt**: Questions own abilities and worth

## Life Lessons

The primary lesson for Life Path 2 is learning to:
1. Stand up for themselves while maintaining peace
2. Trust their intuition
3. Find balance between giving and receiving
4. Develop inner strength
5. Value their own contributions

## Career Paths

Life Path 2 thrives in supportive roles:
- Counselor or therapist
- Mediator or arbitrator
- Human resources specialist
- Diplomat
- Nurse or caregiver
- Social worker
- Administrative assistant
- Musician or artist
