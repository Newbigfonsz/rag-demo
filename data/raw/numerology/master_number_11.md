# Master Number 11: The Spiritual Messenger

## Overview
Master Number 11 is the first of the master numbers, representing spiritual illumination and intuitive insight. People with Life Path 11 carry the qualities of 2 (cooperation, sensitivity) amplified to a higher spiritual octave. They are channels for spiritual information and inspiration.

### The 11/2 Duality
Life Path 11 contains the energy of both 11 and 2:
- **11 energy**: Visionary, inspirational, idealistic
- **2 energy**: Cooperative, sensitive, diplomatic
- Challenge: Living up to 11's potential while grounded in 2's practicality
