# Life Path Number 4: The Builder

## Overview
Life Path 4 represents stability, hard work, and foundation building. People with this life path are practical architects of their lives who create lasting structures. They are meant to learn discipline and develop their organizational abilities.

## Core Characteristics

### Strengths
- **Practical**: Grounded and realistic approach
- **Hardworking**: Dedicated and industrious
- **Reliable**: Trustworthy and dependable
- **Organized**: Systematic and methodical
- **Loyal**: Committed to people and projects
- **Patient**: Willing to work toward long-term goals

### Challenges
- **Rigid**: Difficulty adapting to change
- **Stubborn**: Resistant to new ideas
- **Pessimistic**: Can focus on limitations
- **Workaholic**: May neglect personal life
- **Controlling**: Wants things done their way

## Life Lessons

The primary lesson for Life Path 4 is learning to:
1. Balance work with play
2. Embrace flexibility within structure
3. Trust the process of life
4. Build on solid ethical foundations
5. Allow spontaneity and change

## Career Paths

Life Path 4 thrives in structured roles:
- Engineer or architect
- Accountant or financial planner
- Project manager
- Builder or contractor
- Systems analyst
- Military or law enforcement
- Surgeon or specialist
- Real estate developer
