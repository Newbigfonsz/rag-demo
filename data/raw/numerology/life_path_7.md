# Life Path Number 7: The Seeker

## Overview
Life Path 7 represents wisdom, introspection, and spiritual seeking. People with this life path are natural philosophers and researchers who seek deeper truths. They are meant to learn faith and develop their analytical and intuitive abilities.

## Core Characteristics

### Strengths
- **Analytical**: Deep thinkers and researchers
- **Intuitive**: Strong inner knowing
- **Wise**: Profound understanding
- **Contemplative**: Thoughtful and reflective
- **Perfectionist**: High standards for quality
- **Spiritual**: Natural connection to the divine

### Challenges
- **Isolated**: Tendency to withdraw
- **Critical**: Harsh judgment of self and others
- **Secretive**: Difficulty sharing feelings
- **Cynical**: Can become bitter or skeptical
- **Detached**: Seems cold or distant

## Life Lessons

The primary lesson for Life Path 7 is learning to:
1. Balance intellect with faith
2. Trust intuition alongside analysis
3. Open up emotionally to others
4. Find spiritual truth within
5. Share wisdom without superiority

## Career Paths

Life Path 7 thrives in research roles:
- Scientist or researcher
- Philosopher or theologian
- Psychologist or analyst
- Writer or academic
- Computer programmer
- Investigator or detective
- Technical specialist
- Spiritual teacher
